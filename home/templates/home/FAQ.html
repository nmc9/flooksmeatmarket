What is a basket?

A basket is a feature that you can use to make a shopping list for when you come into the store. You can’t place an order online but you can’t set the basket up with a date you would like to pick up the products in your basket.

What is Location?

The location that the basket refers to is in the store. There locations are the Freezer, the Cooler, in the Beef/Chicken Case, Pork Case, Cold Meat Case, and the Snack Rack. If the location is Ask then you will have to ask an employee in store to get it for you.

Why is there no Price?

We can’t display some prices because the price changes depending on the market. If you would still like to get a pricing please call or email us and we will give you the current price.
